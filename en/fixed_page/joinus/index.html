Would you like to join the group?

We at TRENDcreate, with our catchphrase "Easy to understand, easy to use.
We are working to deliver "easy to understand" to everyone.

Group Participation Requirements

  • Junior high school students and above
  • Have had some experience with programming or have some knowledge of it
  • Have never engaged in problematic behavior such as vandalism,
    and are trusted by others not to engage in vandalism
  • You can join a group and have fun with the members.

Please submit your participation application only if you meet all of the above requirements.
If you belong to another group, please submit your application for participation according to the conditions of each group.

Group Participation Test Flow and Things to Note

  • Participation requests can only be sent via email.
  • After sending the email, you will be hired or interviewed via twitter DM in a few days.
  • After you are hired, an email will be sent to you with a link to join the Discord server.
  • If you are not selected, you will not be notified by email or other means.
  • In order to avoid being rejected based on the content of the email, please write the content in detail.
